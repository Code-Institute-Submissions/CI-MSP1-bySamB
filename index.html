NEW! Brow Lamination

This latest eyebrow treatment involves a two-step process that corrects and sets the direction of the brow hair. In the first step a cream is applied that breaks down the hair bonds so they can be manipulated into the new, desired shape. In step two, a second cream is applied to rebuild the hair bonds and set in the desired shape. Perfect for those with flat, down-growing hairs who are looking to change the direction of the brow whilst also taming any unruly hairs for a more uniformed shape. Brow Lamination will also help achieve a full and fluffy appearance to your brows if sparseness is your dilemma.
